CHALMUN'S

Bar Menu

Welcome to Chalmun's Cantina, where you can get out of the suns, and escape the oppressive heat. Cool down with a cold drink from our eclectic selection of potent potables or munch on our tasty selection of appetizers.

    Brews

  • Alderaanian Ale - $44

    An extremely rare, fermented alcoholic ale, brewed from fragrant grains once native to Alderaan. It had a smooth, rich flavor

  • Corellian Strong Ale - $9

    A strong take on the Galaxy's favorite style of ale. Fresh shipments of this sweet, medium-bodied ale arrive weekly, directly from Corellia

  • Fermented Fungus Ale - $13

    Not for the faint of heart, this a pungent brew is a favorite of Twi'leks from Ryloth

  • Huttese Ale - $14

    Beware of this potent beverage. At 29% ABV, this ale is as repugnant as it is strong, just the way the Hutts like it

  • Kashyyyk Ale - $6

    Chalmun's own special brew. Strong enough for a Wookie, but tasty enough for a humanoid

  • Kri'gee - $10

    A potent and bitter Mandolorian ale that dates back nearly four millenia

  • Lomin Ale - $8

    Wuher's Lomin Ale is sweet, frothy and served warm. Do not ask for it to be served cool!

  • Rodian Ale - $6

    If you've never tried it, then you probably don't want to get too experimental with this one. An acidic ale that only Rodian's seem to enjoy.

    A rare, technically illegal beverage. It is so strong, just a little taste has been known to put some hair on your chest... and your legs, arms, back and everywhere else. We can serve it, but it's illegal to drink, so if you see an Imperial, drink it up quick, you're on your own.

  • Blue Milk - $3

    Also known as Batha Milk, this rich, creamy beverage offers a refreshing respite from Tatooine's blazing, binary suns
